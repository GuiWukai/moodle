Summer 2017 Course sites
Courses for the Summer 2017 terms are ready and set to be not visible to students. To make your course visible to students, follow the instructions below. Your students will then be able to see your course in their "My Moodle Courses" menu on the MyConcordia portal. 1) Find the Administration block in your course (usually found on the left side of your course page if not docked) and click on Edit settings. 2) Find the Visible setting and change it to Show. 3) Click on Save and display at the bottom of the page.
